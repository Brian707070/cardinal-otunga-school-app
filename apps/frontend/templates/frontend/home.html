🏫 About Us

Cardinal Otunga National School, located in the vibrant community of Mosocho, is committed to providing a holistic education that combines academic excellence with character development. Our mobile-friendly web app helps parents, students, and staff stay connected and informed.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."
– Dr. Ezekiel Okeyo, Chief Principal

  • Academic Performance Tracking

  • Mobile-Friendly Access
